Section 80C — save tax (future)
Once your income exceeds ₹3L, invest here to reduce taxable income by up to ₹1.5L/year: SIP in ELSS funds, PPF (₹500 min), life insurance premium. Your SIP already counts if it's in ELSS.
How to file ITR-1 step by step

1. Go to incometax.gov.in → Login with PAN

2. e-File → Income Tax Returns → File ITR

3. Select AY 2026-27, ITR-1 form

4. Personal info auto-fills from PAN database

5. Enter salary from Form 16 (Part B)

6. Enter bank interest (check all 3 banks)

7. Deductions: enter SIP/80C amounts

8. Tax computation auto-calculates

9. Verify via Aadhaar OTP

10. Submit — done! You'll get an acknowledgement number.
